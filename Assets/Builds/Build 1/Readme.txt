Controls :
WASD to move, you get small boosts in speed when you start moving (10 in WS direction and 10 in AD direction, half a second per boost to recharge)
Space to jump, jump force scales with current speed
Q and E to Modify gravity strength
Left click to shoot grappling hook to the Red aiming spot, you get a slow passive boost midair (but reduced WASD mobility)
Right click while grappled to Reel in quickly (once)
Shift to enter a burst mode, hold to aim and release to boost (3 boots, each on a 2 second timer)
